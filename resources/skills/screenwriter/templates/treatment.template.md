## TREATMENT REVIEW PRINCIPLES

After filling it in — go through every scene and answer:

1. **Does every scene start with a "because"?** (Sc. N happened because X happened in sc. N-1.)
2. **Does every scene move a value** from + to – or the other way?
3. **Does every scene serve the controlling idea** of the project?
4. **If you cut the scene, does the film change?** (If not — the scene is dead weight.)

If even one answer is "no" — the scene needs a rewrite or a cut.

---

## AUDIT TAGS

When you find a problem — flag it:

- ⚠ **[CAUSALITY]** — the scene does not follow from the previous one
- ⚠ **[VALUE]** — no movement of meaning
- ⚠ **[BIBLE]** — conflicts with the character or world bible
- ⚠ **[PACE]** — too fast / too slow for its function
- ⚠ **[INFO DUMP]** — exposition through dialogue
- ⚠ **[REPEAT]** — repeats something that already happened
