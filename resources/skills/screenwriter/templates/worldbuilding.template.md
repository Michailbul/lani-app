## RULES OF EXPOSITION

When introducing the world to the audience — NOT through dialogue ("as you recall, our magic works like this"). Only through action, objects on screen, consequences.

A good technique: show the system in action → the hero reacts to it as ordinary → the audience understands this is normal here.
