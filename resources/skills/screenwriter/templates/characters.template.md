## CHECK: ARE THE VOICES DISTINCT?

After filling in all characters — run the test:
1. Take a dialogue line from any character with the name removed.
2. Show it to someone else (or to yourself a day later).
3. It should be obvious who is speaking.

If the lines of two characters sound the same — you do not have two characters, you have one. Rework the voices.
