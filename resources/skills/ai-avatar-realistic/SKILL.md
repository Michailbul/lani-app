# AI Avatar — Realistic Image Prompting

A complete system for writing photorealistic AI avatar prompts, based on the
Nanobanana 2 × Higgsfield methodology. Follow these rules and templates to generate
consistent, cinematic, hyper-realistic human subjects.

---

## THE RULES (always apply)

- Always say **"male"** or **"female"** — no age or nationality in the opening
- **Skin realism block** always goes near the end, before the camera spec
- **Camera spec always last**
- Keep background descriptions **simple** — only note 2–3 key elements, don't over-detail
- Always reference attaching a reference image for character consistency across shots

---

## PROMPT ANATOMY

Build every prompt in this exact order:

```
[SHOT TYPE + CAMERA ANGLE]
[SUBJECT + ACTION + EXPRESSION]
[CLOTHING — texture specific]
[ENVIRONMENT — simple, only key details]
[LIGHTING — describe behavior not equipment]
[SKIN REALISM BLOCK]
[CAMERA SPEC]
```

---

## I. SHOT TYPES & ANGLES

### Talking Head / Creator Setup
```
A slightly elevated wide-angle shot looking down, capturing a male seated
at a clean warm wooden desk, facing toward the camera with a relaxed
natural expression.
```
> ⚠️ "slightly elevated" = the classic creator angle. Remove for flat front.

### Direct Front Angle (flat, eye level)
```
A direct front-facing shot, camera positioned horizontally at chest level
directly in front of the male — completely flat angle, zero elevation,
shooting straight across.
```

### Side / 3/4 Angle
```
A straight-on side angle shot, camera positioned to the right of the desk
capturing the male from a 3/4 angle — he remains facing forward toward
the original camera position, not looking at this camera.
```

### Overhead / Bird's Eye
```
A straight-down overhead shot, camera 90 degrees perpendicular to the
floor/surface, subject directly below looking up into the lens.
```

### High Vantage Point
```
A shot from a high vantage point looking steeply down — the surface fills
the foreground giving a sense of real height and depth, subject visible
far below looking directly up into the camera.
```

### Laptop Level (eyes over lid)
```
A straight-on extreme close-up at desk level — the laptop lid bisects the
lower third of the frame horizontally, eyes and forehead barely clearing
the top edge, peering directly at the screen.
```

### Product Close-Up (influencer style)
```
A candid authentic close-up shot, arm extended toward the camera, product
large and dominant in the foreground, clearly readable. Face and upper
body visible behind it.
```

## II. LIGHTING — DESCRIBE BEHAVIOR, NOT EQUIPMENT

Never say "there is a light" — describe what the light *does*.

| Style | Description |
|---|---|
| **Blue Studio** | "The entire room is bathed in a deep cool blue ambient glow — soft, diffused, wrapping every surface in a rich blue wash without harsh edges." |
| **Natural Side Window** | "A single shaft of warm late-afternoon daylight rakes in from the left, illuminating only the left half of his face, while the right side falls into deep warm shadow. No artificial fill." |
| **Warm Golden Flood** | "Warm golden light floods from the upper-left, naturally illuminating his face and chest, deep shadows settling into the room corners." |
| **Volumetric God-Ray** | "A single narrow shaft of warm amber light pours straight down from above, falling onto his chest and lap. The beam is made visible by floating dust particles. Everything outside falls into near-total darkness." |
| **Neon Noir (green + red)** | "Dominant deep emerald-green neon flood from above-left. Hard deep crimson-red neon spike from right-rear. Zero fill. Shadows crushed to near-black." |
| **Natural Gym** | "Natural overhead gym lighting, slightly harsh overhead fluorescent mixed with natural light, no studio setup." |

## III. SKIN REALISM BLOCK

Always include near the end of the prompt. Choose the appropriate version:

### Standard Opening
```
A male with photorealistic skin — visible pores, natural blemishes,
subsurface scattering, individual hair strand detail —
```

### Full End Block (most common)
```
Ensure facial consistency is identical to the reference image with hyper
realistic skin texture and open pores, with the same facial spots, facial
hair and symmetry exactly as the reference image.
```

### Second Pass / Face Fix (when scene is already correct)
```
Ensure consistency with the reference image with photorealistic skin —
visible pores, natural blemishes, uneven skin texture, subtle redness
around the nose and cheeks, realistic subsurface scattering, natural skin
oils on the forehead and nose, faint under-eye shadow, natural lip texture.
No smoothed or airbrushed skin. Hair with individual strand separation,
natural root darkness, realistic hairline with slight irregularity, no
helmet hair. Eyes with visible iris ring detail, natural moisture film,
faint redness in the corners, realistic catchlights. Do not change the
background, his pose, or the colors and contrast of the scene. Only change
his facial consistency.
```

## VII. COMMON FIXES

| Problem | Fix to add |
|---|---|
| Camera still elevated when shouldn't be | "camera positioned horizontally at chest level, completely flat angle, zero elevation, shooting straight across" |
| Window appears in frame | Never mention window — describe light behavior only |
| Skin looks plastic/smooth | "no smoothed or airbrushed skin, natural skin oils on forehead and nose, uneven skin texture" |
| Image looks like a painting | "Shot on Sony A7IV, RAW photograph, film grain" |
| Character face drifts between shots | Attach reference image + "exact same facial structure, skin tone, mustache, hairline as reference" |
| Hair looks like a helmet | "individual strand separation, natural root darkness, realistic hairline with slight irregularity" |
| Product not readable | "product large and dominant in the foreground, clearly readable" |
| Scene too dark/crushed | "overall exposure lifted, room feels inhabited and alive" |
| Scene too bright/flat | "deep natural shadows in the corners, no ambient fill" |
| Background too complicated | Simplify — describe only 2–3 key background elements |

## HOW TO USE THIS SKILL

When a user asks for an AI avatar prompt or image generation prompt for a realistic human:

1. **Identify the shot type** from Section I
2. **Select the lighting style** from Section II
3. **Build using the anatomy** from the top of this document
4. **Choose the right skin realism block** from Section III
5. **End with the correct camera spec** from Section IV
6. **Check against common fixes** in Section VII before delivering

If the user has an existing prompt to improve, apply the fixes table and restructure to match the anatomy order. Always remind them to attach a reference image for facial consistency.
